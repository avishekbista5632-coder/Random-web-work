BIM (TU)

Tribhuvan University's Bachelor of Information Management (BIM) degree is a four-year (eight-semester) program that combines IT and management courses. This course is designed to help students build information technology skills while also teaching them how to grasp professional competence when in decision-making positions.

In order to assess, configure, and use information systems, information management entails the development and use of theories, ideas, models, methodologies, and tools.
